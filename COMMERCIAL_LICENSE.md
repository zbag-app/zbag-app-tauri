- redistributing copies and derivatives under the same terms;

Version 2.0** on its second anniversary. That future grant is irrevocable: the
FSL grant is never withdrawn, and after two years the version is additionally
usable under Apache-2.0 with no restriction on Competing Use.

## When a commercial license is required

The only thing FSL restricts, and only for the first two years of each released
version, is a **Competing Use**: making zbag available to others in a
commercial product or service that substitutes for zbag, substitutes for
another product or service we offer using zbag, or offers the same or
substantially similar functionality.

If you want to make a Competing Use of a version before its Apache-2.0 future
license takes effect, you need a separate written commercial agreement with the
licensor.
